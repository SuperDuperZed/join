FAQ

Who is this server for?

Everyone. Whether you are Anglican, Catholic, Orthodox, simply curious, or somewhere in between, you are welcome. The server is rooted in the broader catholic tradition and embraces all who seek fellowship, prayer, and thoughtful discussion.

Do I need to be religious to join?

Not at all. While the community is grounded in Christian faith and practice, seekers, doubters, and the spiritually curious are warmly welcomed. Theological study and honest questions are encouraged.

What happens in the server?

The community gathers for prayer, liturgical discussion, theological study, fellowship, and general conversation. There are dedicated channels for different topics, and the community is built on mutual respect and charity.

Is there a specific denominational affiliation?

The parish is Anglo-Catholic in character but ecumenical in spirit. Members come from Anglican, Roman Catholic, Eastern Orthodox, and other traditions. The focus is on what unites rather than what divides.
